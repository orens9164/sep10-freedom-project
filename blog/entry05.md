## Skills

The main two skills I've learned from this are how to learn something on my own, and how important it is to try things that you're learning to make sure you understand them.

### Learning on your own

To learn something on your own, there are many ways you can do it. one of the best in my opinion is to search on youtube, and then copy what they do to practice.

### Trying things that you're learning

Sometimes, some people may just watch a video or two to learn something. this is'nt always reliable, because if you don't do it yourself, you might be completely lost.
